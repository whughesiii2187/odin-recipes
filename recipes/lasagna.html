Lasagna

Description

Everybody loves lasagna, and this recipe is the one to top all recipe's. It is so amazingly good

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Marinara Souce
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil Noodles
  2. Brown Meat
  3. Add Meat to Sauce
  4. Layer Noodles / Sauce Until pan is full
  5. Sprinkle with Cheese and bake 45 minutes at 350
