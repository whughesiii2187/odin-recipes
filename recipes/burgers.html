Basic Cheeseburger

The Basic Everyday Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • 3 Pepper Burger Season
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Red Onion
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Mix 4 Tbsp Seasoning into Ground Beef
  2. Form beef into patties
  3. Cook patties in skillet with bacon fat
  4. While Cooking Patties Prepare the Bun by adding Lettuce, Onion, Tomato, and Mustard to top bun
  5. Place Cooked Patty on bottom bun, mount the top bun and Enjoy
