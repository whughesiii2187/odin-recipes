The Ruben Sandwich

This is the best sandiwch ever

Ingredients

  • Rye Bread (2 slices)
  • Corned Beef
  • Saurkraut
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Thousand Island Dressing
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Warm up Corned Beef and Saurkraut Separately
  2. Butter one side of each slice of Rye Bread
  3. Once the Corned Beef and Saurkraut are warm, assemble the sandwich: Bread / Corned Beef / Dressing / Cheese / Saurkraut / Bread
  4. Cook in a heated skillet until both buttered sides of the bread are golden and the cheese is melted
